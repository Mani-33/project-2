The Taj Mahal in Agra is widely considered as one of the most beautiful buildings in the world, and attracts millions of visitors each year. The white marble mausoleum was built in the 17th century by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his late wife Mumtaz Mahal. The intricate carvings and inlaid precious stones in the building are remarkable and visitors are often left in awe of its beauty and grandeur. The surrounding gardens, fountains and reflecting pool also add to the serene and peaceful atmosphere. Despite the large crowds, it is definitely worth a visit to experience the stunning architecture and rich history of the Taj Mahal
