Care and attention

Nurture is the care and attention given to children as they grow and develop. It helps children build relationships, connect with others, and develop a sense of self-worth. Nurture can also mean taking care of, feeding, and protecting someone or something, especially young children or plants.

Grooming

Nurture can also mean the way a person grooms themselves to achieve success. For example, nurture can include mentoring and care a person receives in an environment.

Environmental factors

Nurture can also refer to the external or environmental factors that affect human development, such as how someone is raised, socioeconomic status, early childhood experiences, education, and daily habits. For example, nurture can include a person's family and social relationships, culture, and community.
